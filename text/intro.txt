To understand the root of the misconceptions held by those who criticize pleasure and commend pain, I will delve into the matter and elucidate the statements made by the pioneer of truth and, in a sense, the designer of a fulfilling life.
No one shuns or loathes pleasure because it is inherently unpleasant; rather, they avoid it due to the significant pains that befall those who fail to pursue pleasure with wisdom. Similarly, no one embraces pain for the sake of pain itself, or seeks it out, but there are circumstances where individuals endure pain and hardship in their quest for a greater pleasure.
